DeckFilter

A simple and intuitive filter plugin inspired by the feel of a DJ controller.

← Back to Works Gumroad

Project Overview

DeckFilter is a plugin that emulates the behavior of filters found on DJ controllers. By turning a single large knob, you can achieve intuitive and dynamic filtering effects.

Background

The primary reason for development was hearing reports that some existing DJ filter plugins caused CPU spikes during operation.

Additionally, I was interested in creating a plugin with a very simple and bold design, featuring just one massive knob in the center of the screen, which motivated me to start this project.

Technical Challenges

1. Reproducing Natural Filter Behavior

To emulate a DJ controller's filter, I implemented a mechanism where the Q-value (resonance) changes automatically internally in tandem with the operation of the Frequency (cutoff) knob.

However, simply linking the values didn't result in a musical and natural variation. It took a lot of struggle and fine-tuning to find the 'natural settings (curve)' that felt pleasing to the ear.

2. UI Design of the Giant Knob

To emphasize intuitive operability, I adopted a layout that places a massive knob right in the center of the screen.

It was a challenge to adjust the details, such as textures and drop shadows, to create a design that didn't look overly stretched or unnatural even when displayed at such a large size.

3. Meter Placement Balancing Functionality and Aesthetics

An unnatural empty space was created between the Color knob and the logo section.

To solve this, I added audio meters in these dead spaces. This ingenuity not only improved the functionality of the plugin but also naturally balanced the overall visual appearance.
